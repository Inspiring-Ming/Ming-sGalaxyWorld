About

Photo

PhD graduate from the School of Computer Science and Engineering at UNSW Sydney, specializing in ontology-driven architectures, ESG knowledge management, and fintech applications.

My research focuses on designing knowledge graph systems that bridge the gap between complex regulatory frameworks (SASB, IFRS, TCFD) and practical corporate ESG reporting. I combine semantic web technologies with data engineering and machine learning to build scalable, standards-compliant solutions.

At UNSW, I led Project P002 at the Financial Artificial Intelligence Consortium (FAIC), a cross-disciplinary initiative spanning agile software processes, responsible investment, and generative AI in fintech — in collaboration with industry partners including Cognitivo and Westpac.

OntoMetric: An Ontology-Guided Framework for Automated ESG Knowledge Graph Construction

Mingqin Yu, Fethi A. Rabhi, Boming Xia, Qinghua Lu

Preprint, December 2025

Addresses the challenge that ESG disclosure frameworks (SASB, TCFD, IFRS S2) embed reporting requirements in unstructured PDF documents. Proposes an ontology-guided framework using LLMs for automated knowledge graph construction.

Projects

Featured

ESG Knowledge Graph System

An ontology-driven ESG reporting system built on SASB standards. Uses an RDF knowledge graph (525 triples) to map reporting frameworks, categories, metrics, and calculation models, then computes ESG metrics from real company data (422K+ records across 40+ companies).

  • 4-service architecture: Knowledge Graph, Data Retrieval, Calculation, Report Generation
  • 7 competency questions (CQ1–CQ7) answered via SPARQL
  • PDF and Word report generation with data lineage
  • Covers Semiconductors (TC-SC) and Commercial Banking (FN-CB)

Featured

OntoMetric System

An ontology-guided framework for automated ESG knowledge graph construction using Large Language Models. Processes unstructured ESG standard PDFs (SASB, TCFD, IFRS S2) and transforms them into structured, queryable knowledge graphs.

  • LLM-powered extraction from ESG standard documents
  • Automated ontology population and validation
  • Data engineering pipeline for knowledge graph construction
  • Led project and supervised student contributors
